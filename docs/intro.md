# Introduction

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Learning Platform** - a comprehensive educational resource for mastering cutting-edge robotics technologies.

## About This Course

This platform provides a structured learning path covering essential topics in modern robotics and artificial intelligence, including ROS2, Digital Twins, NVIDIA Isaac, and Vision-Language-Action systems.

### What You'll Learn

Our curriculum is organized into four comprehensive modules:

- **Module 1: ROS2** - Fundamentals of Robot Operating System 2, the backbone of modern robotic applications
- **Module 2: Digital Twin** - Creating virtual replicas of physical robotic systems for simulation and testing
- **Module 3: NVIDIA Isaac** - Leveraging NVIDIA's robotics platform for advanced AI capabilities
- **Module 4: Vision-Language-Action** - Integrating perception, reasoning, and action in intelligent robots

Each module contains weekly lessons with practical exercises, theoretical foundations, and hands-on projects designed to build expertise progressively.

## Getting Started

Navigate through the modules using the sidebar to begin your journey in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. Start with Module 1: ROS2 if you're new to the platform, or jump to specific topics based on your interests and experience level.

## Target Audience

This platform is designed for:
- Robotics engineers and researchers
- AI/ML practitioners interested in robotics applications
- Students pursuing robotics and AI studies
- Developers looking to integrate AI with physical systems
- Anyone passionate about the convergence of artificial intelligence and robotics

Ready to dive into the future of robotics? Select a module from the sidebar to begin your learning journey.
